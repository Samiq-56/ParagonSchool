About Paragon

Established with a vision to blend academic rigor with royal values, Paragon School offers an unparalleled journey from Playgroup to Grade 10. Our crimson and navy legacy stands for courage, wisdom, and holistic growth.

We believe every child is a masterpiece waiting to be discovered. Our faculty of national award-winners and our state-of-the-art campus create an environment where potential becomes reality.

  • Legacy of Excellence since 2005
  • Handpicked Faculty & World-Class Mentors
  • State-of-the-art Safety & Security Protocols
  • Global Curriculum with Pakistani Values
