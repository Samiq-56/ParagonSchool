01

Introduction

Paragon Public High School ("School", "we", "us", or "our"), located at Gulberg III, Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan, operates this website to provide information about our programs, admissions, and academic activities.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information submitted through our website, admission forms, and digital communication channels. By using our website or submitting any information to us, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our website or submit any personal information to us.

02

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with the following applicable laws and regulations of Pakistan:

PECA 2016 — Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act

We operate in compliance with PECA 2016, which governs data privacy, unauthorized access to data, and the protection of personal information in electronic form. We do not engage in or facilitate unauthorized interception, access, or misuse of any user data.

Punjab Child Protection Act 2016

As an educational institution serving minors, we strictly comply with the Punjab Child Protection Act 2016. We take all reasonable steps to ensure the safety, dignity, and privacy of every child enrolled in or associated with our School.

Constitution of Pakistan — Article 14

We recognize the right to dignity and privacy as protected under Article 14 of the Constitution of Pakistan and reflect that commitment in all our data handling practices.

Pakistan Telecommunication (Re-organization) Act 1996

All electronic communications and data transmissions on our platform are handled in compliance with applicable telecommunication regulations.

04

Children's Privacy (Students Under 18)

Paragon Public High School serves students who are minors (under the age of 18). We take the protection of children's data with the highest seriousness and comply fully with the Punjab Child Protection Act 2016.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children without explicit parental or guardian consent.
  • All admission information for students is collected from parents or legal guardians.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose any student personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.
  • We do not display, publish, or distribute photographs, videos, or identifying information of students without written consent from a parent or guardian.
  • Any staff or affiliated persons who misuse student data will be subject to disciplinary action and may be reported to relevant authorities under PECA 2016 and the Punjab Child Protection Act.
⚠️

If you believe that your child's information has been collected or used in a manner that violates this policy, please contact us immediately at the details provided in Section 12. We will investigate and take corrective action within 7 working days.

05

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect strictly for the following lawful purposes:

  • Processing and evaluating student admission applications
  • Maintaining official student and parent records
  • Communicating with parents/guardians about school activities, fees, and academic progress
  • Responding to inquiries submitted through our website or WhatsApp
  • Sending administrative notifications such as exam schedules, results, and school announcements
  • Improving the functionality, security, and performance of our website
  • Complying with legal obligations under Pakistani law
🚫

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, selling to third-party marketers, or any purpose not listed above. We do not engage in profiling or automated decision-making that affects individuals.

06

Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following strictly limited circumstances:

  • Legal Obligation: When required by Pakistani law, court order, or a lawful government authority (e.g., FIA, law enforcement) under PECA 2016 or other applicable legislation.
  • Child Safety: If we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety or welfare of a student or the public, in accordance with the Punjab Child Protection Act 2016.
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase / Google for data storage) who are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality and process data only on our behalf.
  • Educational Authorities: With official education boards (e.g., BISE Lahore, Punjab Education Department) solely for academic or regulatory purposes.
Important — Third-Party Transfers

Any third party with whom we share data is required to handle that data securely and in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy and applicable Pakistani law. We do not share data across international borders except where required by our technical infrastructure (e.g., Google Firebase servers), which is disclosed here transparently.

07

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Use of Firebase Firestore with access controls and security rules to restrict data access
  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Restricted staff access — only authorized personnel can access student data
  • Regular security reviews of our website and data storage systems
⚠️

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting personal information, we will notify affected individuals within a reasonable time and take all steps required by law.

08

Cookies & Website Usage

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the browsing experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g., session management). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics). These are anonymized and do not identify individuals.

You may disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any cookie that sells or profiles user data.

09

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information held by us:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you or your child.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of personal information where it is no longer required for the purpose it was collected, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Object: Object to the use of your personal information for any purpose other than those stated in this policy.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12. We will respond within 14 working days.

✅ Parents and guardians may exercise these rights on behalf of their minor children at any time. No request will be penalized.

10

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law:

  • Admission Applications: Records of enrolled students are retained for the duration of enrollment and for a minimum of 5 years after leaving, in accordance with standard educational record-keeping practices.
  • Unsuccessful Admission Enquiries: Deleted within 6 months if admission is not pursued.
  • Website Inquiry / Contact Form Data: Retained for up to 12 months, then deleted.
  • Technical/Usage Data: Retained for a maximum of 12 months for security and analytics, then anonymized or deleted.

Upon request, we will delete information earlier than the stated retention period unless legally required to retain it.

Photo & Media Consent

The School may photograph or record video during official school events, ceremonies, sports days, and academic activities for educational and promotional purposes.

Default Consent Upon Enrollment

By enrolling a student at this School, parents or guardians grant the School a non-exclusive, royalty-free right to use photographs or videos of the enrolled student taken during official school events for educational and institutional promotional purposes — including the school website, official notice boards, and school social media accounts.

  • Photos and videos will only be used for school-related, non-commercial purposes.
  • Student images will never be shared with third-party advertisers or commercial entities.
  • No student will be individually identified by full name alongside their photograph on public-facing platforms without explicit written parental consent.
  • Parents who do not consent to their child's media use must notify the School in writing at the time of admission or at any point thereafter. The School will honour such requests promptly.

✅ To withdraw media consent at any time, submit a written request to the School office. Your request will be honoured within 7 working days and existing published content will be removed where feasible.

By submitting any form on this website or in person, the parent or guardian confirms that all information provided is accurate, complete, and truthful to the best of their knowledge.
